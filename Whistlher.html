Whistlher!

Welcome to my first Plugin!

over the next few weeks I hope to have the files uploaded so you can try out the instrument- check back here for links

Ok, technically it is a Native Instruments Kontakt sample library but a few months ago this felt completely beyond me and now, it's here!

so what it is? well I've recorded human whistling over roughly 2 octaves and have put these into Kontakt so it means you can ply the whistles on your midi keyboard!

VERY EXCITING!

for me anyway- I'm not sure if it will be visible but over the course of the last few months I have also been learning web design through

Code First Girls

why exactly would I do this on top of doing a Masters in Electronic Music Production? *yes, why?!*

well because like many ADHD'ers I like to understand things inside and out before I feel like I can get it let alone innovate and when I realised there was some coding knowledge required and we had to design our own Graphical User Interface I realised I need to know more

So I've gone from knowing nothing about coding, web development, design or kontakt instruments to making my first instrument and coding my first website- ok it's a bit 2003 BUT! these are things I genuinely was not able to execute 3 months ago

I recommend checking out Code First girls as their aim is to get more women into tech and to do this in small groups in short courses.

the 8 week intro courses are a good start as they are a reasonable commitment - 2 hours per week- and a reasonable pace with a group project to work towards and they then have (mostly) industry sponsored 4 month micro degrees -more intense several evenings a week- you can pursue after if it interests you.
All in all a great way to learn tech skills and reskill into a new or related area.

For me I love tech and music even though I come from a very "untechy" background of being a singer songwriter-
there is something in me that wants to push into the music/tech dissection to make sure it is isn't full of stereotypes- there's room for all of us!
