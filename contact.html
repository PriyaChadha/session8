I'm starting to work out what might be useful for myself and others as we progress

through our music careers and I'm aware that

  1. my experience is limited to Predominantly Inattentive ADHD- for example I don't have problems sitting still so may not instinctively understand what it is like for someone with feelings of physical hyperactivity

  2. obviously the umbrella of neurodivergence is hugely errr divergent! initial investigations and feedback into developing this idea has shown it is probably best to start with what I know and go from there but it is hoped that in gaining as many insights as possible we will be able to work out how more neurodivergent people can build and sustain a career in music without compromising their uniqueness

  3. one of the things that has been clear from survey answers thus far as that people want to know how to harness or hack their neurodivergent characteristics to make their lives easier- this is something I'm working on

Please get in touch either by email or by participating in the survey linked here- I'm really happy to hear from you and need your views to help shape something that works
